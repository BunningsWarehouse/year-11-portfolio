Overview

For this project, me alongside my other group members are going to make a text-based adventure game written in python.

















End Users

My end users are teenagers aged 13-19. They will want a game that isn't too short as it will keep them entertained for longer. They will prefer for the game to have multiple paths to take/options and variety with enemies so they have more control over the game.



Requirements

  • Characters
    • The ability to customize your character
    • Options for your character's stats
    • Multiple side characters
  • Story paths
    • Paths effect gameplay and story
    • Multiple paths for multiple endings
    • Extra side stories within different paths

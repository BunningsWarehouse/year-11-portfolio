Overview

For this project, I will be making a Low-Poly character model for use in a platformer game reminiscent to Super Mario 64.

















End Users

My end users are children aged 7-13 years old. Chidren this age will want a game with more of a challenge, but still simple enough to be able to comprehend the game, so I will add more of an intricate movement system. They would prefer smaller, cuter designs so my character will be simple. They will also want more animation in the character, so I will make multiple animations for each movement (e.g animations for lightly running, normally running and running at full velocity).



Requirements

  • Legs
    • Smaller size, relatively the size of the arms
    • Peg-legged type design with shoes
    • has two segments to make proper animations
  • Eyes
    • Simple, it would look terrifying if the eyes were realistic
    • Oval shaped
    • Black and White
  • Arms
    • Less detail in the arms
    • Hands are like small spheres
    • has two segments to make proper animations
  • Torso
    • Slightly larger in height than the arms or legs
    • Hands are like small spheres
    • Wearing a Shirt/T-Shirt
  • Animation
    • Cartoonish movement
    • Simplistic animation, but with a little more detail
    • Utilization of particle effects, such as small dust clouds forming behind the character while running
    • Purposely lower-framerate as part of aesthetic
  • Rigging
    • Bones for each limb
    • Multiple bones in the arms and legs to allow them to bend
